About BISU Bilar Campus...

Bohol Island State University - Bilar (BISU - Bilar) is a state-owned university campus located in Bilar, Bohol. The school is one of the campuses part of the Bohol Island State University (BISU), formerly known as Central Visayas State College of Agriculture, Forestry and Technology.

The Bilar campus provides undergraduate and graduate degree programs in Education, Agriculture, Allied Sciences, and Technology. In addition to tertiary programs, the school also has a senior high school program offering strands under the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track.

The university, as well as its programs, are duly recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).
