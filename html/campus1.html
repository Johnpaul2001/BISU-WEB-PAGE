About BISU Balilihan Campus...

Bohol Island State University - Balilihan (BISU - Balilihan) is a public university campus located in Balilihan, Bohol. The school is one of the five (5) external campuses that comprise the Bohol Island State University (BISU) System. The establishment of the campus was brought upon by the initiative of the Congressman of the First District, Hon. Edgar M. Chatto with the aim to provide a tertiary school in their locality.

Academic programs that are offered in Balilihan Campus are mostly composed of Technological courses. It offers degree programs in Information Technology, Electronics Technology, Electrical Technology, Computer Science, Criminology, and Industrial Technology with major in Food Preparation and Service Management.

Significantly, these academic programs offered in Balilihan Campus are duly recognized and accredited by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).
