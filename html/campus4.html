About BISU Clarin Campus...

Bohol Island State University - Clarin (BISU - Clarin) is a public university campus located in Clarin, Bohol. The school is one of the campuses part of the Bohol Island State University (BISU) System. The campus traces back its root as a school of fisheries that offers vocational program under the Supervision of Bureau of Vocational Education in 1962.

Currently, the Clarin campus has seven (7) academic programs with diploma, undergraduate, and graduate degrees. Courses in Technology and Allied Sciences and Teacher Education make up its undergraduate and diploma studies while its graduate study program provides a graduate study in Education with a major in Education Management. These academic programs offered in Clarin Campus are duly recognized and accredited by the Comisssion on Higher Education (CHED).
